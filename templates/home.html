About the System

What is TCCS?

The Toxic Comment Classification System (TCCS) is designed to identify harmful, offensive, or inappropriate comments in online platforms. It uses machine learning algorithms trained on real-world datasets to classify comments as toxic or non-toxic.

This system can assist social media platforms, forums, and online communities in maintaining healthy digital environments.
